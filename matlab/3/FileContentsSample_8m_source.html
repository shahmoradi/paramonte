35 %> ``slfc`` : The scalar MATLAB integer representing the column
36 %> index of the dataframe component ``df`` that contains
37 %> the natural logarithm of the objective function values

39 %> such that the following relationship holds.<br>
40 %> \code{.m}
41 %> FileContentsSample.ndim = FileContentsSample.ncol - FileContentsSample.slfc;
42 %> \endcode
43 %> While this column index can be readily inferred by exploring
44 %> the contents of the dataframe component, this column index is also
45 %> computed and explicitly offered to conveniently slice the values of
46 %> the sampled states and their corresponding log-function values.<br>
